Welcome to Monarda Taxonomy

This website is dedicated to the study and classification of the genus Monarda, featuring species profiles with detailed descriptions and microscope images of herbarium specimens.

About Monarda

The genus Monarda, belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae), is known for its aromatic and medicinal properties. Commonly known as bee balm, these plants are native to North America and are highly valued for their vibrant flowers and ecological importance.
